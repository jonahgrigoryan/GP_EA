Equal Opportunity Employer Statement

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Equal Employment Opportunity

Advanced Wound Care & Solutions is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We are committed to creating and maintaining a workplace that is free from discrimination and harassment. We believe that diversity and inclusion strengthen our team and enhance the quality of care we provide to our patients.

Non-Discrimination in Employment

Advanced Wound Care & Solutions provides equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants for employment without regard to:

  • Race
  • Color
  • Religion
  • Sex (including pregnancy, childbirth, and related medical conditions)
  • Sexual orientation
  • Gender identity or gender expression
  • National origin
  • Age (40 and older)
  • Disability (physical or mental)
  • Genetic information
  • Veteran status or military status
  • Marital status
  • Citizenship status
  • Any other characteristic protected by federal, state, or local law

Scope of Policy

This policy applies to all terms and conditions of employment, including but not limited to:

  • Recruitment and hiring
  • Training and development
  • Compensation and benefits
  • Promotions and transfers
  • Performance evaluations
  • Disciplinary actions
  • Termination of employment
  • All other aspects of employment

Harassment-Free Workplace

Advanced Wound Care & Solutions is committed to maintaining a work environment free from harassment based on any protected characteristic. We do not tolerate harassment of any kind, including verbal, physical, or visual harassment, and will take appropriate action against any employee who violates this policy.

Reasonable Accommodations

We are committed to providing reasonable accommodations to qualified individuals with disabilities and to employees with sincerely held religious beliefs, unless doing so would cause an undue hardship. If you require an accommodation, please contact our office to discuss your needs.

Retaliation Prohibited

Advanced Wound Care & Solutions strictly prohibits retaliation against any employee or applicant who files a complaint of discrimination or harassment, participates in an investigation, or opposes discriminatory practices. Any employee who engages in retaliation will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

Reporting Violations

If you believe you have experienced or witnessed discrimination, harassment, or retaliation in violation of this policy, please report it immediately to:

Compliance with Laws

Advanced Wound Care & Solutions complies with all applicable federal, state, and local laws regarding equal employment opportunity and non-discrimination, including:

  • Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964
  • The Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
  • The Age Discrimination in Employment Act (ADEA)
  • The Genetic Information Nondiscrimination Act (GINA)
  • The California Fair Employment and Housing Act (FEHA)
  • All other applicable federal, state, and local laws

Our Promise

At Advanced Wound Care & Solutions, we believe that our diverse team of healthcare professionals is our greatest asset. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where every team member can thrive and contribute their unique skills and perspectives to providing exceptional patient care.
