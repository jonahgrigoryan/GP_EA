Non-Discrimination Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Statement of Non-Discrimination

Advanced Wound Care & Solutions is committed to providing high-quality wound care services to all individuals in our service area. We comply with applicable Federal civil rights laws and do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, age, disability, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other characteristic protected by law.

Our Commitment

Advanced Wound Care & Solutions:

  • Provides services to all patients regardless of their background
  • Treats all patients with dignity and respect
  • Does not exclude people or treat them differently because of race, color, national origin, age, disability, sex, sexual orientation, or gender identity
  • Provides free aids and services to people with disabilities to communicate effectively with us, such as qualified sign language interpreters and written information in other formats (large print, audio, accessible electronic formats, other formats)
  • Provides free language services to people whose primary language is not English, such as qualified interpreters and information written in other languages

Accessibility Services

If you need these services, please contact our office at (833) 316-3030 or awcsmedicalgroup@gmail.com. We will work with you to ensure you can access our services and communicate effectively with our care team.

Section 1557 of the Affordable Care Act

Advanced Wound Care & Solutions complies with Section 1557 of the Affordable Care Act (ACA), which prohibits discrimination on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, age, or disability in certain health programs and activities.
